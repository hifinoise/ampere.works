Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't. The T1E is designed with intention.

📺

ePaper Display

Crystal clear in direct sunlight and uses minimal power. Your screen stays on without draining your battery.

🔋

4+ Days Battery Life

With our target of 16 days using partial refreshes. Charge less, live more.

🔧

Fully Hackable

100% open-source with easy access to components. Modify, extend, and make it truly yours.

🕒

Real-Time Clock

Accurate timekeeping with ESP32Time and WiFi connectivity to fetch time when needed.

😴

Deep Sleep Mode

Intelligent power management with hibernate features for maximum battery life.

🎨

Custom Fonts

Express your style with customizable typography. Because Times New Roman just doesn't cut it.
